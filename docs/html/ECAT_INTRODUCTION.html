Introduction

The EtherCAT SubordinateDevice Controller (ESC) hardware abstraction layer (HAL) drivers and subDevice examples are designed to operate on the EtherCAT hardware peripheral on the following C29X devices:

  1. F29H85X

The F29H85X devices support EtherCAT on CPU1.
